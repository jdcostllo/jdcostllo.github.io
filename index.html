Cambridge, MA

I am a mechanical engineering student at MIT, pursuing a masters of science in mechanical engineering.

I graduate in August 2024 and am seeking new roles!

My research is on the design of renewable-powered, water desalination systems which utilize electrodialysis, a separation process based on semipermeable membranes ion-exchange membranes and electrical fields.

I’ve done a little of everything and I am always looking to do more. I feel right at home programming a CNC mill, operating a lathe, writing ladder logic for programmable logic controllers, configuring variable frequency drives, programming in MATLAB and Python, troubleshooting electronics, making extensive parametric CAD models, and just about everywhere in between. In this portfolio, you’ll find a smattering of content from my research, work, and school projects as well as the numerous personal projects I’m constantly hacking through.
